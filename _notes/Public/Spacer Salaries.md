Aside from a handful of job charts, Mothership doesn't offer a good guideline for how much characters should be paid per gig. Sean McCoy made the following recommendation on <a href="https://discord.com/channels/461670627468771329/478391156498104341/809879288102125598">the Mosh Discord</a>:

<blockquote>
  <p>Here's something to try, players get paid a month's salary for a job based on their skills:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>trained skills: 500cr a month</li>
    <li>expert skills: 1,000cr a month</li>
    <li>master skills: 2,000cr a month</li>
  </ul>
  <p>Dangerous jobs (probably most) pay 2x. Suicidal/impossible jobs pay 3x.</p>
</blockquote>
